ABOUT

ABOUT

Welcome to Hack Genesis '25, the premier hackathon that sparks innovation and creativity! As we step into our latest edition, Hack Genesis '25 continues to bring together some of the brightest minds from across the globe for a 36-hour marathon of ideation, coding, and problem-solving.

For those new to hackathons, they are events where teams collaborate to create software or hardware projects within a short timeframe, typically one or two days. Participants work together to brainstorm ideas and build something innovative, such as apps, websites, AI models, drones, or anything wild your imagination can dream up. It’s an exciting opportunity to learn, share skills, and bring creative concepts to life.

Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a curious newcomer, Hack Genesis '25 welcomes you to join us on this incredible journey. The future is ours to shape—let's seize this opportunity and hack our way to a brighter tomorrow. 🌍
